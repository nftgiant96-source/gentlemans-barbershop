Our Story

Craftsmanship
You Can Trust

Gentlemen's Barber has been London's trusted destination for precision grooming since our founding. With three convenient locations across the city, we bring the classic barbershop experience to every neighbourhood — delivering sharp cuts, expert shaves, and the timeless style every gentleman deserves.

Our skilled barbers combine traditional techniques with modern styles, ensuring every client leaves looking and feeling their best. Whether you're after a classic taper, a skin fade, or a hot towel shave, we have you covered.

Our Services

Premium grooming tailored to your style

Men's Haircut

Classic and contemporary cuts tailored to your face shape and lifestyle. Every cut includes a wash, cut, and style.

Fade & Taper

Precision fades from skin to scissor cut, blended seamlessly for that clean, sharp finish only an expert barber can deliver.

Beard Trim

Expert beard shaping and trimming to keep your facial hair looking clean, defined, and well-groomed every time.

Hot Towel Shave

Head Shave

Straight Razor Shave
